FIT CLUB FAQS

A few of the most commonly asked questions about Fit Club.

WHAT’S THE ‘FIT CLUB’?

The MyFitFoods Fit Club is simple. You pay $10 a month and save 20% on all of your MFF purchases and can cancel any time you want.

IS THIS A CONTRACT?

This is not a contract. We don't like contracts. Pay as you go, stop anytime you'd like. xoxo

IS THERE A MONTHLY LIMIT ON SAVINGS?

Nope. We don’t cap your monthly spend or once you reach a certain amount of savings because that would just not be cool. You can literally buy as much MFF snacks and meals as your belly desires and it’s all 👏🏼 twenty 👏🏼 percent 👏🏼 off 👏🏼.

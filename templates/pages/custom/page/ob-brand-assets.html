Brand Resources

Below you’ll find everything you need to know about the MyFitFoods brand and guidelines to ensure its correct use.

-

Our Name

Use the correct spelling, casing, and spacing when referring to our brand, meals, and/or products.

MyFitFoods, not “My Fit Foods” or “Myfitfoods”.

-

Our Logo

You may use our logo in reference to our products, but avoid using it in contexts where it could be mistaken for an official endorsement or offering.

Logo Guidelines

Let it breathe. Our logo shouldn’t feel crowded by its surrounding elements. As a rule of thumb, it should have at least as much space on all sides as the width of the “Fit” in MyFitFoods.

Let it scale. If you’re using our logo on the web, please use the SVG files when possible.

Don’t mess with it! We like our logo just the way it is, so please don’t:

  • Enlarge or shrink any part of the logo.
  • Disproportionately scale, skew, shear, stretch, or flip the logo.
